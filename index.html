Rootgrass Communities

Empowering Minority Ownership, Land Restoration, and Cultural Revival

Our Mission

Rootgrass Communities aim to restore ancestral lands, ensure minority ownership in critical industries, and build sustainable ecosystems guided by the principles of Nova and powered by Aurelium.

Key Features

Land Reclamation

Reclaim ancestral lands through reparations, negotiations, and legal advocacy.

Minority Ownership

Empower minority communities by promoting ownership in agriculture, energy, and cultural exports.

Aurelium Currency

A decentralized economy tied to land, cultural products, and sustainable industries.
